Coaxial swerve, is the far more straightforward way to do a drivetrain. For movement, it is simply powered by 1 motor per pod, with a single servo to power azimuth rotation.

Pros:
"simpler"
"easier to program"
4 wheels, which enables complete drivetrain and more traction
fits into a parallel plate packaging
more modular
cons:
more current draw
takes up 4 servo slots
