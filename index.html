INTRODUCTION

Have you heard the story of Darth Veer the wise? Its not a story that they would tell you

SwerveDrives are one of the most complex and difficult mechanisms in FTC, in an area where improved performance is often underapreciated. It can be difficult, and is far more time consuming than the typical Mecanum drivetrain. Many a team have tried, and failed to get it to work. It is almost unilaterally advised not to attempt. Compared to the time invested, mecanums are far better, and are consistently viable in every game, unlike swervedrives that vary in viability from season to season. Yet, the fact that you're here suggests you seek something greater.

Swerve is a pathway to many abilities some consider to be ... unnatural...

On the other hand, when they do work, Swervedrives can be absolutely broken. Kneebreaking maneuvers, increased traction, insane speed, extreme defensive and counter defensive properties all come with the package of a working swervedrive. They can enable absurd teleops and push the ceiling of autos.

enough budget

BEFORE WE BEGIN

Make sure that swerve is even viable in the current season. For a comeptition that is movement heavy, such as centerstage or DECODE, a swervedrive might be the deciding factor in a match. However, in seasons where the ideal robot eliminates movement entirely, such as Into The Deep, swervedrives are almsot useless. while the aura is undeniable, its not nearly as worth it if the game isnt movement based.
